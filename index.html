About Evergreen

Evergreen Work Solutions was founded by healthcare professionals who understand the documentation burden facing today's medical providers. Our mission is to alleviate that burden through technology-enabled services that put patient care back at the center of medicine.

With over 10 years of experience in medical documentation, our team combines clinical expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver accurate, timely, and compliant documentation services.
